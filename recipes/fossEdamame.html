FOSS Edamame

Description

The Foss edamame is an amazing protein source. In my opinion, edamame on its own taste great. If you live in the US, you can find edamame in the frozen section of your supermarket.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag frozen edamame
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • a splash of lemon or lime juice
  • (optional) soy sauce or tamari (gluten free) for added flavor

Steps

  1. Boil the Edamame:
    • Bring a pot of salted water to boil. Add the frozen edamame and cook for about 4-5 minutes, until they are tender. Drain them in a colander.
  2. Sauté (optional for Extra Flavor):
    • in a skillet, heat 1 tbsp olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add minced garlic (if using) and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
    • Toss the boiled edamame into the skillet and saute for 1-2 minutes to infuse them with the oil and garlic.
  3. Season:
    • Sprinkle with salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes.
    • You can also drizzle with soy sauce or tamari for savory umami flavor.
    • For a fresh twist, squeeze some lemon or lime juice over the top right before serving.
