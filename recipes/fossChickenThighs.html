FOSS Chicken Thighs

Description

The FOSS chicken thighs are really flavorful, not difficult to follow making, and are relatively cheap that can last you over a week for meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 4.75-6 pounds of chicken thighs with skin
  • A good amount of olive oil (more on that later)
  • 2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • A good amount of Grill Mates Montreal Chicken Seasoning (more on that order)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Prepare the Chicken:
    • Pat the thighs dry with a paper towel. This helps them crisp up.
  3. Season:
    • In a small bowl, mix olive oil, cayenne pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, salt, and a good amount of Grill Mates Montreal Chicken Seasoning. (No I am not sponsored by them) Rub this mixture all over the thighs to coat them evenly.
  4. Bake:
    • Place the thighs on a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil. Bake for an hour, turning them halfway through for even cooking.
  5. Crisp Up:
    • Increase the oven temperature to 450 degrees fahrenheit for the last 10 minutes to crisp up the skin.
  6. Rest:
    • Let the thighs rest for 5 minutes before serving.
