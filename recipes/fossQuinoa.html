FOSS Quinoa

Description

The FOSS quinoa pairs well with a variety of dishes and can also stand on its own for consumption. A good amount of nutrition provided in every grain of FOSS quinoa.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups quinoa (rinsed)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • a sprinkle lemon zest (or lemon pepper)
  • 1 tsp parsley

Steps

  1. Cook Quinoa:
    • Combine the quinoa and broth in a pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover. Cook for 15-20 minutes,or until the quinoa has absorbed all of the liquid.
  2. Fluff and Season:
    • Once cooked, fluff the quinoa with a fork. Stir in olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, lemon pepper, and parsley.
